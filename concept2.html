One hundred years of
baking for Barbados.

We didn't just start a bakery. We became part of the island's breakfast. From one small oven in 1923 to one of the largest bakeries in Barbados, our story is Barbados' story — told through warm bread, loyal customers, and a community that has shaped everything we bake.

During this time our range has grown from a simple sliced loaf to a full family of local favourites: sweet pastries, seasonal bakes, dinner rolls, and our ever-popular healthy whole-wheat range. Same heart. Same ovens. Same island.

Our Heritage

We've been part of your morning since 1923.

"We didn't just start a bakery. We became part of the island's breakfast."

Zephirin's Bakeries has been rising with Barbados since 1923. From the first family loaf to one of the largest bakeries on the island, we've baked the bread that feeds the people — at the breakfast table, in the school lunch box, and on every corner store shelf.
