Our Story

Over a Century of
Bajan Baking Heritage

In 1923, Zephirin founded a small bakery with a simple belief: bread should nourish families, taste beautiful, and be made with honest ingredients. One hundred years later, that spirit still rises in every loaf that leaves our ovens.

From a single oven in Bridgetown to six parishes across Barbados, Zephirin's has grown — but our roots, our recipes, and our people have never changed.

1923
Founded in Bridgetown Zephirin opens a small family bakery, baking by hand each morning before sunrise.
1967
Tudor Bridge Flagship A landmark store opens at Tudor Bridge, becoming a Bajan institution.
1993
10 Grain Loaf Introduced Our signature loaf launches and becomes Barbados' best-selling artisan bread.
2023
100 Years Celebrated A century of baking fresh, baking Bajan — celebrated island-wide.

"Fresh bread is not a luxury — it is the warmth of home, the kindness of morning, the taste of where you belong."

— The Zephirin Family, Bridgetown, Barbados
