Our Heritage

Over a century of baking for Barbados

We didn't just start a bakery. We became part of the island's breakfast. From one small oven to one of the largest bakeries on the island — our story is Barbados' story.

1923
The Beginning Zephirin's Bakery is founded in Barbados, bringing fresh-baked bread to the island community for the first time.
1960s
Growing with the Island As Barbados grew, so did Zephirin's. New products, new customers, the same trusted quality every morning.
1990s
Expanding the Range Whole wheat, bran, and health-conscious options introduced — baking for every Bajan lifestyle.
Today
One of Barbados' Largest Bakeries 100+ years on, still baked with the same heart. Stocked in stores across the whole island every day.
