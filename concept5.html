A Bajan Legacy
Generations Deep.

For over a century, Zephirin's Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Barbadian community. What started as a small family oven in Bridgetown has grown into the island's most beloved wholesale and retail bakery.

"We don't just bake bread; we bake the memories of Sunday mornings, community gatherings, and the authentic taste of home."

Our commitment to traditional slow-baking methods and local ingredients ensures every loaf of salt bread and every sweet bun carries the warmth of Bajan culture.
