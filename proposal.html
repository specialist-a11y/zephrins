The Opportunity

Your website should work as hard as your bakers do

Zephirin's Bakery has been feeding Barbados since 1923. Over 100 years of heritage, island-wide distribution, and a product every Bajan knows — yet the current website underserves the brand at every turn.

The site relies on decorative snow particle effects, an unconfigured WooCommerce shop, and inconsistent hours information. There's no clear path for a customer to find where to buy your bread.

This redesign fixes all of that. The strategy is simple: tell the Zephirin's story beautifully, show the product compellingly, and make it effortless to find a stockist.

01
Convert browsers into store visitors A prominent "Find Our Bread" CTA on every page, backed by an island-wide store locator
02
Tell 100 years of story Dedicated heritage section with a visual timeline that builds pride and trust
03
Showcase the full product range Filterable product gallery so customers can discover every loaf before they shop
04
Load fast and look great on mobile Remove 200KB of unused WooCommerce code and optimise for Barbados mobile connections
