The Zephirin Legacy

Since 1944, our family has been the keepers of the island's morning ritual. What began as a humble stone oven by the shore has grown into a pillar of the community, yet our recipe for the perfect crust remains unchanged.

"The secret isn't just the flour, it's the Atlantic air and the hands that work the dough before the sun rises." — Elias Zephirin, Founder.
