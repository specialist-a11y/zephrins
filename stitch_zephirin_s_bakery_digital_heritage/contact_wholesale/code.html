Wholesale FAQ

Everything you need to know about partnering with Zephirin's.

Our standard minimum for island-wide delivery is $150 BBD. For smaller orders, pick-up at our main bakery in Bridgetown is available starting from 6:00 AM.
While our main facility focuses on heritage wheat breads, we have a dedicated section for gluten-sensitive bakes including cassava-based specialty breads. Contact our product team for custom allergen-safe development.
For next-day delivery, orders must be placed via our portal or phone by 2:00 PM. This ensures our bakers have enough time to prepare your dough for the overnight rise.
