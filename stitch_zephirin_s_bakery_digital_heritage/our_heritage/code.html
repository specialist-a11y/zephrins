A Legacy Baked in Tradition

From a small family kitchen in Bridgetown to a Caribbean icon, our story is one of resilience, flavor, and the warmth of the island sun.

Our Bajan Roots

It began with a secret recipe for Coconut Sweet Bread and a passion for the perfect crust. In the heart of Barbados, Zephirin’s was born from the belief that bread is more than sustenance—it's a celebration of family.

We honor our heritage by using the same traditional stone ovens and local ingredients that our founder, Samuel Zephirin, championed seven decades ago. Every loaf tells the story of the Bajan soil and the spirit of our community.
