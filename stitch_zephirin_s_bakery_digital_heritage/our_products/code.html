Bread Care & Availability

How should I store my bread?

For optimal freshness, keep bread in a cool, dry place. Our Salt Breads are best enjoyed the day of purchase, but can be revived with a quick 5-minute toast at 350°F.

Is your bread available island-wide?

Yes! Zephirin’s is stocked in most major supermarkets across Barbados and local community shops. Look for the orange logo in the bakery aisle.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Currently, our heritage recipes focus on traditional wheat and whole-grain bakes. We are exploring ancient grain alternatives for future collections.
