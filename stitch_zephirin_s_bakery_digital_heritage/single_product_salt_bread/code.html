Authentic Bajan Salt Bread

A crusty outside, a fluffy inside, and just the right touch of sea salt. The soul of Barbadian mornings since 1954.

The Legend of the Roll

Crafted using a generations-old sourdough starter, our Authentic Bajan Salt Bread isn't just a staple; it's a piece of Barbadian history. Known locally for its distinctive savory crust and airy crumb, each roll is hand-kneaded by our master bakers in the heart of Bridgetown.

We use locally sourced sea salt from the rugged Atlantic coast, giving it that unmistakable "salt-of-the-earth" flavor that pairs perfectly with everything from local ham to simple melted butter.
