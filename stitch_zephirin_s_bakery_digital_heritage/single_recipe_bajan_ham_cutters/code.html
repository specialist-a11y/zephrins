Bajan Classic

Bajan Ham Cutters using Salt Bread

schedule 15 MINS PREP
restaurant 5 MINS COOK
bar_chart DIFFICULTY: EASY

What You Need

  • bakery_dining

    4 Fresh Salt Bread Rolls

    Zephirin’s Bakery Signature Crust

  • egg_alt

    500g Picnic Ham

    Thickly sliced, leg-off

  • local_fire_department

    Bajan Hot Sauce

    Traditional yellow mustard-base

  • nutrition

    Slivers of Aged Cheddar

    Optional, for extra richness

“The secret is the crust. If it doesn't crunch when you squeeze it, it isn't a cutter.”

— Uncle Zephirin

Method to the Magic

1

Prep the Bread

Using a serrated knife, slice the Salt Bread horizontally, but not all the way through—leave a 'hinge' to keep the juices inside.

2

The Ham Foundation

Layer 2-3 thick slices of warm picnic ham. For the true heritage experience, ensure the ham has a slight glaze of brown sugar and cloves.

history_edu

Heritage Baking Tip

Our Salt Bread is fermented longer to achieve that 'crunch and air' texture. Never microwave the bread; a quick 2-minute toast in a 350°F oven restores its morning freshness perfectly.

3

The Finishing Touches

Drizzle the Bajan Hot Sauce generously. Add your aged cheddar if using, and press the cutter firmly with the palm of your hand before serving.
