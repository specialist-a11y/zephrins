Frequent Questions

Which stores carry Zephirin's?

We partner with all Massy Stores locations, Carlton Supermarkets, and select boutique delis and local convenience stores across Barbados. Use the map above to find the one closest to you.

What are your delivery schedules?

Fresh batches are delivered daily between 6:00 AM and 8:30 AM to ensure you have the freshest bread for breakfast. Second drops occur at major supermarkets on Wednesday and Friday afternoons.

Can I order directly for home delivery?

Currently, we focus on wholesale distribution to our retail partners. However, select items are available via local delivery apps from our partnered delis.
