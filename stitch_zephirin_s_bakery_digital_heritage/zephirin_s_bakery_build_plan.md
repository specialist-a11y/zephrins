# Zephirin’s Bakery - Website Design Plan

## 1. Home Page
*   **Hero:** Immersive parallax with floating Salt Bread and Sweet Buns. "Fresh Bajan Bread, Baked with Heritage."
*   **Heritage Preview:** A two-column story section with a warm image carousel.
*   **Featured Products:** A 3-column grid featuring the core range (Salt Bread, Sweet Buns, Whole Wheat).
*   **Trust Section:** "Why Bajans Love Zephirin's" with icon-based value props.
*   **Store Locator Preview:** A map-inspired graphic guiding users to "Find a Retailer".
*   **Social Proof:** A testimonial carousel of happy local families.

## 2. Our Heritage (About Us)
*   **Mini Hero:** Atmospheric imagery of the bakery's roots.
*   **Timeline:** "Our Journey Through Time" detailing the bakery's growth in Barbados.
*   **Team/Community:** Introducing the hands behind the bread and their Bajan roots.
*   **Bakery Gallery:** A masonry grid of behind-the-scenes warmth.

## 3. Our Products (Product Catalog)
*   **Product Grid:** High-fidelity cards for every loaf and bun, including ingredients and traditional serving suggestions.
*   **Product Info:** Detailed accordions for nutritional facts and storage tips (keeping it Bajan-fresh).
*   **FAQ:** Addressing common questions about wholesale and islandwide availability.

## 4. Where to Buy (Store Locator)
*   **Interactive Map:** A clear, easy-to-use search for finding Zephirin’s across the island's parishes.
*   **Retailer Cards:** Lists of supermarkets and local shops with directions.

## 5. Recipes & Community
*   **Recipe Cards:** Creative ways to use Salt Bread and Sweet Buns (e.g., Bajan Ham Cutters, Bread Pudding).
*   **Baking Tips:** Expert advice on enjoying bakery products at their best.

## 6. Contact & Wholesale
*   **Wholesale Portal:** A dedicated form for businesses, shops, and restaurants to partner with the bakery.
*   **Contact Info:** Direct lines and location for general inquiries.
